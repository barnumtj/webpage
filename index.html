    • About Me

    I was born and raised in the great state of Nebraska. I lived there my whole life up until about 6 months ago. While in Nebraska I participated in track and basketball throughout high school. I continued to run track in college which was a great experience for me and taught me many things. After leaving college I wasn't sure what my next step was and moved back home. During these years I worked several jobs including serving at restaurants and working for Frito-Lay. After realizing I didn't enjoy these type of jobs I have now decided to pursue a career in web development.

    I have several hobbies that include fishing, sports, gaming, and working out. I started fishing a couple years ago and have been obsessed ever since. My favorite sports teams include the Nebraska Cornhuskers, Kansas City Chiefs, Cleveland Cavaliers, and Sporting KC. When it comes to gaming I enjoy first person shooters and sports games. If I'm not doing one of the three things above then I am most likely working out. I find it to be not only great for my physical health but my mental health.
